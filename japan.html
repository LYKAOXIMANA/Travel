Cultural Highlights

Japan offers a unique cultural experience where ancient Shinto shrines stand beside futuristic skyscrapers. Traditional tea ceremonies, sumo wrestling, and kabuki theater coexist with cutting-edge technology and pop culture.

Japanese cuisine, or washoku, is renowned for its emphasis on seasonal ingredients and beautiful presentation, with sushi, ramen, and tempura being famous worldwide.

Travel Tips

Get a Japan Rail Pass for unlimited travel on JR trains

Remove shoes when entering homes, temples, and traditional restaurants

Carry cash as many places don't accept credit cards

Try an onsen (hot spring) experience but note tattoos may be restricted

Top Attractions

Mount Fuji

Japan's iconic sacred mountain and highest peak, offering breathtaking views especially during cherry blossom season.

Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kyoto

Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates that trail through the forest up Mount Inari.

Tokyo Skytree

The tallest structure in Japan offering panoramic views of Tokyo from its observation decks.
