Cultural Highlights

Italy is home to more UNESCO World Heritage Sites than any other country. From Renaissance art in Florence to ancient ruins in Rome, the country is a living museum.

Italian cuisine varies by region but is universally celebrated for its pasta, pizza, gelato, and espresso.

Travel Tips

Use Italy's extensive train network to travel between cities

Dinner is typically served later (8-10 PM)

Tipping is not expected but appreciated for exceptional service

Validate train tickets before boarding to avoid fines

Top Attractions

Colosseum, Rome

The iconic amphitheater that once hosted gladiator contests and public spectacles in ancient Rome.

Venice Canals

Explore the romantic waterways of Venice by gondola or vaporetto (water bus).

Florence Cathedral

Marvel at Brunelleschi's magnificent dome dominating the skyline of Renaissance Florence.
