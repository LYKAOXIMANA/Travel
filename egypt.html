Egypt

Step back in time to explore ancient wonders and experience rich cultural heritage

Back to Countries

Quick Facts

Capital: Cairo

Population: 104 million

Language: Arabic

Currency: Egyptian Pound (EGP)

Best time to visit: October-April (cooler months)

Cultural Highlights

Egypt is home to one of the world's oldest civilizations, with a history spanning over 5,000 years. The country is famous for its ancient monuments, including the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, and the Valley of the Kings.

Egyptian culture is a blend of many influences, including Pharaonic, Greek, Roman, Coptic Christian, and Islamic traditions.

Travel Tips

Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites

Bargaining is expected in markets (souks)

Check photography rules at historical sites

Drink bottled water and stay hydrated in the heat

Top Attractions

Pyramids of Giza

The last surviving of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, consisting of the Great Pyramid, Pyramid of Khafre, and Pyramid of Menkaure.

Luxor Temple

A large Ancient Egyptian temple complex located on the east bank of the Nile River in the city of Luxor (ancient Thebes).

Nile River Cruise

A journey along the world's longest river, offering views of ancient temples and rural life along its banks.
