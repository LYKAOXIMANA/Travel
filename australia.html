Australia

Discover the land of stunning landscapes, unique wildlife, and vibrant cities

Back to Countries

Quick Facts

Capital: Canberra

Population: 25.7 million

Language: English

Currency: Australian Dollar (AUD)

Best time to visit: September-November, March-May

Cultural Highlights

Australia is known for its laid-back lifestyle, diverse culture, and love for sports and the outdoors. The country has a rich Indigenous history dating back over 65,000 years.

Australian cuisine reflects its multicultural population, with influences from Europe, Asia, and beyond. Don't miss trying a traditional Aussie barbecue or meat pie.

Travel Tips

The sun is intense - always wear sunscreen, hat, and sunglasses

Distances between cities are vast - plan travel time accordingly

Water conservation is important in many parts of Australia

Be aware of bushfire warnings during summer months

Top Attractions

Sydney Opera House

One of the world's most famous performing arts venues and an architectural masterpiece on Sydney Harbour.

Great Barrier Reef

The world's largest coral reef system, home to an incredible diversity of marine life and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park

Home to the iconic Uluru (Ayers Rock), a massive sandstone monolith sacred to Indigenous Australians.
